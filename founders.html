FAQ

Questions You're
Probably Thinking

Can complete beginners with no tech background apply?
Absolutely yes. You do not need to know any coding, design, or tech. Dominal Founders is built exactly for founders who have the business idea and the drive but not the technical skills. We handle 100% of the technology side once you're selected.
Can teams participate, or only individuals?
Both are welcome. You can apply as an individual founder or as a team. If applying as a team, mention all team member names in the application. We recommend teams of 2–4 people for startup ideas that require multiple skill sets.
Are AI-based startups allowed?
AI-enabled startups are welcome — but copy-paste AI wrappers are not. If your idea uses AI as a smart tool to solve a real business problem with a clear revenue model, it will be considered. Generic "ChatGPT for X" ideas without a real business angle will be rejected.
Is there any registration fee or cost involved?
Zero. There is no registration fee, no application cost, and no hidden charges. The entire program — including technology, hosting, domain, website, and app — is provided free to selected founders as part of the partnership.
Who owns the startup idea after submission?
You own your idea 100%. Submitting does not transfer any rights to Dominal. If selected, a transparent partnership agreement is signed where ownership terms are clearly defined. Your startup, your business — Dominal is the technology partner.
What exactly happens after selection?
After selection, Dominal contacts you directly for an onboarding call. You'll discuss the product scope, features, branding, and launch timeline. Dominal then begins building your website and app. The full process from selection to launch typically takes 4–8 weeks depending on the complexity of your startup.
How many startups will be selected?
There is no fixed number. Dominal selects based on quality, not quantity. If 10 genuinely strong startup ideas come through, all 10 could be selected. If only 2 are truly strong, only 2 will be chosen. We prioritize quality partnerships over volume.
